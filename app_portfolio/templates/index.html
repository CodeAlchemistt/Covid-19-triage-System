// SYSTEM OVERVIEW

CLINICAL
INTELLIGENCE
AT SCALE

Ritaved is a real-time clinical decision support engine built on a Random Forest machine learning model. It ingests a COVID-19 patient's demographic data and pre-existing comorbidities, then generates a precise mortality and ICU admission risk score within milliseconds.

Unlike broad population studies, Ritaved operates at the individual patient level — giving emergency physicians and triage nurses an objective, data-driven second opinion precisely when time is most critical.

The underlying dataset was sourced from the Mexican Secretariat of Health (SSA), encompassing over one million confirmed COVID-19 cases with complete clinical outcome records.

INSTANT TRIAGE

Submit a patient's vitals and comorbidities via the intake form. The Random Forest model returns a risk score and STABLE / CRITICAL verdict in under one second.

14 COMORBIDITIES

The model analyses pneumonia, diabetes, COPD, hypertension, cardiovascular disease, obesity, and 8 additional clinical risk factors simultaneously.

FULL ANALYTICS

EDA charts, confusion matrices, ROC curves, and feature importance rankings are all documented in the Analytics page for full transparency.

// PIPELINE

HOW IT
WORKS

01

INTAKE

Clinician submits patient age, sex, institution type, and all 14 comorbidity flags via the Triage Form.

02

NORMALISE

The Flask API preprocesses the payload — encoding categoricals, handling missing values, and scaling features to match training distribution.

03

INFER

The trained Random Forest model runs inference across 500 decision trees, returning a mortality probability score from 0 to 1.

04

VERDICT

The dashboard instantly displays STABLE (green) or CRITICAL (red), along with the exact probability and a clinical recommendation.
